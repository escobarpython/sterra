STERRA is a living space where creativity, code, and rhythm coexist. built from a collective dream, it connects young talents, restless minds, and bold ideas. through every project, the collective redefines what it means to create together, combining experimentation, emotion, and purpose. from digital labs to rehearsal rooms, every corner of STERRA vibrates with imagination.

DEV is the technological backbone of the collective. behind several local awards and digital innovations, the dev team gathers aspiring and emerging developers who are shaping their craft line by line. they explore new codes, build immersive experiences, and bridge creativity with technology, turning ideas into interactive realities.

RECORDS is where the sound of STERRA takes shape. led by the producer saintkarl and the rappers jonvarer, ytotk, lil homer, and young pi, the sessions are built on collaboration and constant learning. between late night jams and studio experiments, they refine skills and transform ideas into records that carry feeling and intent.

SOUND carries the pulse of raw energy. behind the garage band censored, led by jonvarer, this division explores the edges of noise and harmony. the mission is to test the limits of texture, dynamics, and emotion, bending expectations while searching for the perfect imperfection.
